The Tipping Point Game

In this experiment you will see paper tubes of different sizes (like from the center of toilet paper or paper towel rolls).

Your job is to tilt each tube such that it reaches the "tipping point": The angle at which the paper tube will begin to tip over.

The tube will be sitting on a table, and the direction of the tilt is indiucated by an arrow. Use the arrow keys to change the angle of the tipping point, then press the space bar.

There will be 32 trials. Try to guess the anlge of each tipping point!

Use to tilt the tube in the direction indicated.

When you think you have found the tipping point, press SPACE BAR.
